Bli Med

For attendees without their own gear, we have a selection of nylon swords, masks and gloves available such that everyone who are interested can try their hand in exercises and sparring!

The beginners course is free. We encourage you to come and try!

The membership fee is 250kr each year. This is kept relatively low as many of us are students and we want to encourage interested members to aquire gear over time.
