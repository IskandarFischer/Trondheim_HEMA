About us

Trondheim HEMA Club

Trondheim HEMA exercising the ever more popular Historical European Martial Arts (HEMA). This is a common term for older European fighting techniques, reconstructed from written sources. HEMA as a whole allows for a large variety of weapons; longswords, sabre, sword and buckler, rapier, daggers, and larger two-handed swords.

The club has chosen longsword as its primary area of focus, where both beginner and advanced courses are created for longsword practice. For longsword we are primarily using Joachim Me√øer as our source, however the club is promoting exploration in various techniques.
Due to the diversity of weapons available there are many members with an interest to learn these. Therefore we have exercises where members can learn and explore the weapon of their choice!

Trondheim HEMA was created in 2015, and has since the autumn that year held regular exercises. We've seen an increase in active members and members with full gear, and these numbers continue to rise!
